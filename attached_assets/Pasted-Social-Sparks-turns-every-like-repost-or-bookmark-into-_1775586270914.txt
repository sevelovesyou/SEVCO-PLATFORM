Social Sparks turns every like, repost, or bookmark into a real economic signal that rewards creators with usable Sparks they can spend on AI generation, music priority, wiki tools, etc.
It creates a virtuous cycle: great content → more Sparks from the platform → more AI-powered creativity → even better content.
All while staying 100% compliant and feeling generous to users.
How Social Sparks Works (User Flow)
1. User sees a post in the feed
2. They click the ⚡ Spark button 
    * Cost to user: 0 Sparks (free action)
3. Instant visual feedback
    * Bolt emoji lights up with a particle effect
    * Post shows new Spark⚡️count
4. Platform processes the reward
    * SEVCO adds 1 Spark to the author’s balance from the central Creator Reward Pool(shown in CMD, Sparks)
    * The action is recorded in the audit log
5. Creator receives immediate value
    * Balance increases instantly
6. Public visibility
    * Post shows total Sparks received(all-time, just how “likes” work)
    * Author’s profile has a “Top Sparked Posts” section highlighting their most-supported content

Profile Feature –
Every user profile gets a new section at the top (after bio):
“Top Posts”
* Shows the 3–5 posts that received the most Sparks this month
* Beautiful card layout with bolt count and thumbnail
* Bookmarked posts get a special glowing border and priority placement
Feed Enhancements
* Each post card shows a small “⚡ X Sparks” counter (updates live)
* Top Sparked posts get a subtle glowing border in the feed
Economic Model (Platform-Mediated)
* Creator Reward Pool is funded automatically whenever users buy Spark packs. Example: 10% of every Spark purchase goes straight into the reward pool.
* If the pool ever runs low, SEVCO can top it up manually or via a small percentage of all revenue.
* This keeps the system sustainable and generous — creators get rewarded even if the sparker is on the free tier.
Safeguards Built In
* Rate limits: max 10 Sparks sent per user per day
* Anti-spam: users can only spark a particular post once
* Full audit log in spark_transactions with type: 'social_reward'
* Platform can pause or adjust reward amounts instantly if needed
Why This Version Wins
* 100% Stripe compliant — no peer-to-peer transfer of purchased credits
* Zero risk of negative money holes — platform controls the outflow
* Feels generous to both creators and fans
* Encourages high-quality content without forcing users to spend
* Scales beautifully as the platform grows
* Unique to SEVCO — no other social network rewards creators this directly with usable AI credits
This turns the entire social layer into a living economy that directly powers creativity.
